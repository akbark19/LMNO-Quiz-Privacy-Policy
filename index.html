Privacy Policy for QuizIt

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how QuizIt ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application QuizIt (the "App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name (chosen by you for your profile)
  • Authentication credentials (securely managed through Google Sign-In or Amazon Cognito)

2. Game Data

To provide our quiz services, we collect:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Quiz performance statistics
  • Achievement and level completion data
  • In-app purchase history
  • Coins and power-ups inventory
  • Lives and game session data

4. Purchase Information

For in-app purchases, we collect:

  • Purchase transaction details
  • Payment verification data (processed through Google Play Store)
  • Purchase history and receipts

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Provide quiz game services and features
  • Maintain your game progress and statistics
  • Process and verify in-app purchases
  • Manage your account and authentication

Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and game content
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Provide customer support

Communication

  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Notify you about new features or content

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

Your game data is securely stored using:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure
  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication through Amazon Cognito and Google Sign-In

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

Your Rights and Choices

  • Update: You can modify your profile information at any time
  • Delete: You can request account deletion by contacting us or using our Data Deletion Request Form

Data Control

  • Data Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Data Deletion: Request removal of your personal data using our Data Deletion Request Form

Communication Preferences

You can opt out of non-essential communications through app settings.

Children's Privacy

QuizIt is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Our app offers in-app purchases including:

All purchases are processed through Google Play Store's secure payment system. We do not store your payment information.
